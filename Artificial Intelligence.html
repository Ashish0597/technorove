Artificial Intelligence


The science of mimicking or simulating human intelligence through machines,
computer programs, and robots to perform a variety of tasks is called Artificial Intelligence or AI.

Magic of Natural Language Processing (NLP) in Artificial Intelligence


is the branch of Artificial Intelligence that helps computers read, understand, and interpret human languages.
NLP uses language interpretation to facilitate interactions between humans and computers.
Every day, massive volumes of data are generated in an unstructured form by businesses. Also called free-text, such data poses a significant challenge for businesses to analyze and interpret. NLP can identify key phrases and categories present within the free-text to derive meaning from even the most jumbled-up structures of data.
